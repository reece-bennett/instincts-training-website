We are Instincts Training

Instincts Training Ltd was founded in 2022 with the aim of providing bespoke training to a range of organisations

Paul Bell Director of Training

With over four decades training in the martial arts, the last 15 years in street self-defence under one of Europe’s leading self-defence/personal safety experts and one of his personal students, (Dave Turton founder of SDF/ASMAA associations and Andy Crittenden, founder of ACMAC Martial Arts).

A degree in Psychology, (BSc Hons), and my own personal experience from real life past encounters, I will pass on knowledge to keep you and your employees/students safe with the confidence to avoid a dangerous situation. And, if all else fails, simple but effective self-defence techniques to make a quick effective escape.

Dawn O’Hara Director of Marketing and Business

Highly experienced professional with over 30 years’ experience across multiple roles in a range of organisations. A persuasive leader responsible for planning, organising, prioritising objectives within a business organisation, able to adjust my management style to suit different environments. Colleague and customer engagement is a priority and evidence of robust talent and succession plans in place. Determined, tenacious, organised, self-motivated approach to all aspects of my role including customer service, operational demands, trading legally, budgeting effectively, also delivering annual growth.

I am very interested in well-being and making a difference everyday. People and inclusion are very important to me so I will be bringing that passion into our daily way of working. Having a life balance is also a big factor and this now feels achievable with the creation of Instincts. The original idea for Instincts came as a result of feedback over the years that I should work for myself, so finally having the knowledge, confidence and skills, Instincts was founded.
